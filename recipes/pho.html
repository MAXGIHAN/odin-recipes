Pho Recipe

Description

Pho is a fragrant Vietnamese noodle soup, known for its flavorful broth made from simmered beef bones, aromatic herbs, and rice noodles. Perfect as a warming, refreshing meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) beef brisket or flank steak
  • 1 onion, halved
  • 2-inch piece of ginger, sliced
  • 4-5 star anise
  • 3-4 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 8 oz rice noodles
  • Fresh herbs (cilantro, basil, mint)
  • Bean sprouts
  • Lime wedges
  • Hoisin sauce and sriracha sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare Broth: Char onion and ginger in a dry pan. Add to a pot with beef broth, star anise, cloves, cinnamon, sugar, and simmer for 30-45 minutes.
  2. Cook Noodles: Cook rice noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Cook Beef: Slice beef thinly and briefly cook in the broth (or use raw beef for thin slices in the soup).
  4. Assemble: Place noodles in a bowl, pour broth over the top, and add beef slices.
  5. Serve: Garnish with fresh herbs, bean sprouts, lime wedges, and optional sauces.
