Lasagna Recipe

Description

This simple lasagna is a crowd-pleaser with layers of tender noodles, savory beef, rich marinara, and melted cheese. Quick to make and full of flavor, it’s perfect for family dinners or gatherings!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or sausage
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce (or your favorite pasta sauce)
  • 9 lasagna noodles (cooked according to package instructions)
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil or 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven & Prepare Pan: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13-inch dish.
  2. Cook Meat: Brown the ground beef or sausage, then stir in marinara sauce. Simmer for 5-10 minutes.)
  3. Mix Cheese: Combine ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan, egg, and basil in a bowl.
  4. Assemble Lasagna: Layer sauce, noodles, cheese mixture, and mozzarella in the dish. Repeat, finishing with sauce and cheese.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Uncover and bake for an additional 10 minutes. Serve: Let sit for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
