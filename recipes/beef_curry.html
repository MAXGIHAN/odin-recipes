Beef Curry Recipe

Description

A rich, flavorful, and aromatic dish, Sri Lankan Beef Curry is packed with bold spices like cinnamon, cardamom, and curry leaves. This hearty curry pairs perfectly with rice or roti.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef, cubed
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-3 cinnamon sticks, 3-4 cardamom pods
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Salt, pepper, and 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh herbs (cilantro, basil, mint)
  • Bean sprouts
  • Lime wedges
  • Hoisin sauce and sriracha sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Saut√© onion, garlic, and curry leaves in oil.
  2. Brown beef, then add spices and tomatoes.
  3. Add coconut milk, water, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 1-1.5 hours until beef is tender.
  4. Serve with rice or roti.
